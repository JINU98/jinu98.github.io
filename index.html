About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of South Carolina, working in the ICAS Lab under the supervision of Prof. Ramtin Zand. My research focuses on optimizing Large Language Models (LLMs) for deployment on resource-constrained edge devices, with an emphasis on quantization, hardware acceleration, and efficient AI systems.
